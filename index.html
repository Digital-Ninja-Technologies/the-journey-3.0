About the event

A platform built for people building something.

The Journey 3.0 is a dynamic business conference designed to empower Small and Medium-sized Enterprises — a powerful platform for visibility, networking, and access to funding and business knowledge.

Following The Journey 1.0's focus on career development and The Journey 2.0's push into entrepreneurship and enterprise growth, this third edition carries the theme Becoming — turning the ideas, pitches and connections in this room into the business you're building toward.

What happens at The Journey 3.0

Four ways the day works for you — pick one, or do all four.

01

Showcase your business idea

Exhibitions and presentations that put emerging enterprises in front of an audience that matters — visibility for the ideas you've been building quietly.

02

Pitch for real funding

Live pitching sessions where selected SMEs present directly to funders — and can walk away with genuine financial support for their business.

03

Learn from industry leaders

Panel discussions with people who've done it — practical, strategic business knowledge you can apply on Monday morning.

04

Meet the right people

Deliberate networking that connects entrepreneurs with investors and funding organisations — the room is the point.
